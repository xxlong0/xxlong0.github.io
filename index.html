About Me

I obtained Ph.D. degree at the Univeristy of Hong Kong (HKU), advised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura. Before that, I did my bachelor's degree in Control Science & Engineering at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Junfeng Wu.
My research lies in 3D Generative Models, and I am also interested in Neural Rendering, 3D Reconstruction, AI for Robotics etc.
We are organizing AnySyn3D, a research interest group that conducts various topics about the 3D. We could host multiple remote/onsite internships, with topics i) 3D generation, ii) Gaussian Splatting, iii) 3DV for Robotics, iv) Autonomous Driving. If you are interested in working with us, feel free to drop me an email.

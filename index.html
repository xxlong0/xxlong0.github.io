About Me

Tenure-Track Associate Professor at School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Nanjing University. I obtained Ph.D. degree at the Univeristy of Hong Kong (HKU), advised by Prof. Wenping Wang (IEEE & ACM Fellow) and Prof. Taku Komura. Before that, I did my bachelor's degree in Control Science & Engineering at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Junfeng Wu. I received the Excellent Young Scholars Fund (Overseas) from NSFC.

Reseach

My research interests lie in computer graphics and 3D computer vision, with a focus on 3D Vision, Physical AI, and World Models. The long-term goal is to develop General-Purpose AI with spatial capabalities.
